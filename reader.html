Arrival Into The Void

The sky cracked open like glass. Cities fell silent as the gates of void appeared across Earth. No one understood what was happening… except him.

A lone survivor stood in the ruins, hearing a voice whisper inside his mind: “You are the chosen one… Void King.”

Darkness swallowed everything. And the real story was just beginning.
